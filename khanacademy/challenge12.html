Recipe:

  • Time:1 day
  • Serves:30 min
Ingredients Quantity
cream 1 l
flavour(vanilla) 20 gr
chocolate chips 300 gr

Instruction: You need to whip cream till it will turn into heavy consistency.Then add some vanilla, and whip it again. in the end add some chocolate chips and put into freezer or a day.

Recipe:

butter 50 gr
